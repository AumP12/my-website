How To Play

  1. Press Start Game — a random color appears for 5 seconds.
  2. The swatch goes dark. You have 10 seconds to reconstruct it with the H / S / L sliders.
  3. Hit Submit Guess (or let the timer run out) — you score up to 100 points based on how close you are.
  4. Colors get trickier as your streak builds. See how high you can go!
